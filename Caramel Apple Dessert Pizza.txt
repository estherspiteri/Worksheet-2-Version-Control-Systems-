Ingredients:

For the Crust:

125ml hot water
4g instant yeast
28g Tablespoons brown sugar
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
180g all purpose flour

For the Apple Filling:
295g apples, peeled and finely diced
16g flour
28g brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg

For the Streusel:
60g cup flour
115g cold butter
67g brown sugar
45g oatmeal

Instructions:

1.To make the crust, combine hot water and yeast in a large bowl. Let sit for a few minutes until a foam develops. 
2.Stir in the brown sugar, olive oil, salt and flour. Knead for a few minutes until dough is stretchy. Let sit for 30 minutes to rise.
3.Meanwhile, peel and dice the apples. 
4.Toss them with the flour, brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg. 
5.Grease and lightly dust a pizza pan with cornmeal. Preheat oven to 400 F. Pat out the dough into a 12 - 15 inch circle. Lightly brush with olive oil. Spread the apples on top of the dough.
6.To make the streusel, mix together the flour and brown sugar. 
7.Cut in the cold butter until it makes pea-sized crumbs. 
8.Gently stir in oatmeal. Sprinkle over the apples.
9.Bake at 400 F. for 28-22 minutes or until the apples are tender and the crust is browned. Let cool slightly. Serve warm with caramel and vanilla ice cream.
